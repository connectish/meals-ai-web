Meals AI Telegram Bot for Food Recognition & Macro Tracking

Meals AI is a smart nutrition assistant that works inside Telegram. By sending a picture of your meal to the Telegram bot, you get real-time AI-generated estimates of calories, macros, and portion sizes. It’s like having a fitness-savvy friend with a built-in food scanner.

The bot uses computer vision and deep learning to recognize food items from images. It's designed for gym goers, casual health enthusiasts, and anyone looking to track their meals without the hassle of manual logging.

No apps to install. Meals AI lives on Telegram and responds instantly with estimates, meal stats, and diary entries. It works globally and supports payment through Telegram Stars, making it easy to analyze your meals at roughly $0.08 per use.

Whether you’re on a cutting phase, bulking season, or just want to be more aware of what you eat, Meals AI makes food tracking as simple as snapping a photo.
